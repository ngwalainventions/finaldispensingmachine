Services

Production of Organic Pesticides and Fertilizer

We use organic wastes such as garbage and fruit peels from markets, households, and farms mixed with botanical extracts to form two-in-one organic pesticides and fertilizers (Ngwala Organics). The product is produced without energy consumption using complex natural enzymatic processes, microbes, and green nanotechnology. The low-cost Ngwala Organics serves as the best and most effective organic pesticide and fertilizer for sustainable farming compared to the usual chemical pesticides and fertilizers. The product is environment-friendly and produced with high technology, having a shelf life of more than 2 years and a very high concentration compared to other organic pesticides and fertilizers in the market. It supports human safety, soil fertility, increasing plant and soil capacity to retain water, and conserving biodiversity, resulting in human safety, high yields, and climate sequestration.

Digital organic fertilizer and pesticide dispensing machine

Designing, manufacturing, and deploying Ngwala Smart Delivers at a large scale is used to deliver high-quality organic pesticides and fertilizers to small-holder farmers on demand, pay as you farm, and at the lowest cost. The machines are connected to a mobile app to allow farmers to track expenditures, quantities, and impacts. The system is a substitute for the traditional way of distributing pesticides and fertilizer using packages or containers. It delivers high-quality organic pesticides and fertilizers and provides easy access to farm inputs, allowing farmers to pay via mobile money and receive the exact quantities and refill containers for zero waste farming. Also provides access to credit services for farmers, particularly young people and women with limited financial resources, to purchase farm inputs in cash.

Concern

Climate and Evironment

Climate change and pollution from farm chemicals and plastic waste/containers have been on the rise, resulting in human poisoning, water and land pollution. We produce environment friendly, low cost organic pesticides and fertilizer, replacing chemical farm inputs. Its distribution is done using Ngwala Smart Delivers, which allow farmers to refill their canisters.

Smart Logistics

Smart Logistics: Last mile delivery of farm inputs has been a challenge to farmers especially when comes to poor condition of rural infrastructures. Ngwala Inventions uses large trucks over small ones (economies of scale) for distributing organic pesticides and fertilizer to refill our Smart Delivers. Our innovative smart logistics platform allows us to efficiently deliver organic farm input to farmers on the farm at the lowest cost while reducing carbon emissions.

Pay as you Farm Technology

Farmers want high-quality organic farm inputs, but the high up-front cost gets in the way. Our smart delivery technology empowers them to conveniently pay as they farm with their mobile money account.

Farm Better

We work hard day and night to offer farmers high quality organic pesticides and fertilizer at lowest cost, on time, on farm, on pay as farm, on demand and on credit as the substitute of chemical farm inputs and traditional way of distributing using containers. We are on our way to switch 100 million farmers in Africa from using chemical farm inputs purchased from Agro-shops located in Town very far from farms by the year 2025
